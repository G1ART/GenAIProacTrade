## Principles

1. **Replay** shows only what happened and what was knowable at each time — no hypothetical framed as historical fact.
2. **Counterfactual Lab** is explicitly hypothetical — visually and in copy separated from Replay.
3. **Premium** means precise, calm, low-noise — not flashy retail terminals.
4. **Decision quality** (process at time T) ≠ **outcome quality** (aftermath); both may be labeled.
5. **No future leakage**: replay event copy must not cite information that only existed after that event’s time.

## Plot

- Shared time **X**; series differ by color, opacity, stroke (solid/dashed), markers, optional band.
- Default timeline may use an **illustrative** reference series when live prices are absent — must be labeled as non-authoritative.

## Micro-brief

- Structured panel: time, stance/message/evidence snippet, founder note, “known then” line — not a raw browser tooltip.
